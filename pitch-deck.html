How It Works

1. Intelligent Intake

Every lead is automatically scored using 5 adaptive factors: engagement, recency, source quality, contactability, and course fit.

2. Predictive Intelligence

ML models predict conversion probability, progression likelihood, and enrollment ETA for every applicant.

3. Smart Automation

AI detects blockers, suggests next actions, and automates communication with personalized email composition.

4. Continuous Learning

The system learns from outcomes, optimizing scoring weights and predictions weekly based on real conversion data.
